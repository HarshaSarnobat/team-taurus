🔮 What is Probability?

Probability is like guessing the chances of something happening.
In the wizarding world, it's like asking: “What’s the chance Ron forgets his wand again?” 🤔

Example: If there are 3 chocolate frogs and 1 Bertie Bott’s Every Flavour Bean,
Probability of getting a frog = 3 out of 4 = 0.75 🐸
